Bringing the Universe to Wisconsin was a two-year project from 2012-2014 funded by the Ira and Ineva Reilly Baldwin Wisconsin Idea Endowment. This public program brought researchers and staff from the Wisconsin IceCube Particle Astrophysics Center to every two-and four-year University of Wisconsin system campus and community to share science with Wisconsin residences.

After two and a half years, 6,350 miles, 300 volunteers, and 52 events in 26 communities, Bringing the Universe to Wisconsin shared scientific exploration and innovation with over 5,000 Wisconsinites.
