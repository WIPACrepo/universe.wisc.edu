Wisconsin Connection

The Wisconsin IceCube Particle Astrophysics Center (WIPAC) is Wisconsin based, but supports a variety of projects around the world in conjunction with dozens of international Universities and research centers.

Although research dollars are spread around the world, it is estimated that over 100 million dollars were spent in Wisconsin on our largest project, the IceCube Neutrino Observatory.
